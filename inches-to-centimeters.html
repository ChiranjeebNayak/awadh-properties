What is Inch?


Inch is a unit denoting length and is used throughout both the US and Imperial systems of measurement. The inch is defined as equivalent to around 25.4 millimeters as per the official definition issued in the year 1959. A foot contains 12 inches while a yard has 36 inches. Inch is a measurement unit derived from uncia, the Latin term, which works out to 1/12th of a Roman foot. The inch has witnessed various standards earlier and the current definition is based upon the global yard. One of the earliest definitions is based upon barleycorns while an inch is equal to length of three grains of round, dry barley. Another version may have been derived from width of a human thumb. In this case, length was derived from averaging of the width of three thumbs, namely a medium, large and small one. 

What is Centimeter?


A centimeter is a symbol denoting length in the SI or International System of Units, the present avatar of the metric system. It is defined as equivalent to 1/100 meters. The centimeter is based upon the SI meter and as indicated by the centi prefix, it equals 1/100th of a meter. The metric prefixes hover from factors of 10-18 to 1018 on the basis of the decimal system with the base containing no prefix and containing the 1 factor. A centimeter is equivalent to 0.01 meter or 10 millimeters which is also the same as 0.3937 inches. 

Relation between Inches and Centimeters


The relationship of inches to cm denotes that one inch equals 2.54 cm in the metric system. The distance in centimeters will be equal to the distance in inches multiplied by 2.54. 

How to convert Inches to CM


A centimeter equals 0.3937007874 inches. You have to multiply the figure in inches by 2.54 for getting the figure in centimeter. This is the answer for 1 inch to cm conversion. 1 inch in centimeters is 2.54 cm.

Current Use of Inch


The inch is mostly used throughout Canada, USA and UK along with countries like Japan. It is used for measuring sizes of display screens and related components. 

Current Use of Centimeter


Centimeter is used for diverse applications globally where a smaller denominator is needed for the meter. Height is often measured in centimeters outside of the USA.
