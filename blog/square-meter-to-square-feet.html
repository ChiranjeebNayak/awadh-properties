How to Convert Square Meter to Square Feet?

To convert Square meters to Square feet (sqm to sqft) , you need to multiply with 10.764. For example, you are having 4 square meters of area and you want to convert it into square feet. Multiply 4 with 10.764 euqal to 43.0556 Square Feet

Sqm to Sqft Formula & Example

Square Meter to Square Feet (m2 to ft2) Formula

A square meter to square feet conversion is simple and is done via below formula.

ft² =m² * 10.764

Sq Mtr to Sq Feet (sq m to sq ft) Example

  • 10 square meters is equal to 10*10.764 = 107.64 square feet
  • 50 square meters is equal to 50*10.764 = 538.2 feet
  • 100 square meters is equal to 100*10.764 = 1076.39 feet
  • 200 square meters is equal to 200*10.764 = 2152.8 feet

Relationship between Sq m to Sq ft

Square meter to square foot conversion figures, you should know that 1 square meter is equivalent 10.76391042 to square foot. 1 square foot is equivalent 0.09290304 to square meter. This is how you can readily convert square meters to square foot if you know these figures accurately.

What is a Square Meter (sqm)?

When a plot of land is square and the length of the boundary wall is one meter. To calculate the formula for the plot, you need to multiply two sides and the result is in terms of Squares Meter. This unit cannot be used to measure a single length or a particular direction. A square meter of the area is not equal to a meter square of area. For example, if you have a plot of land with 4m length and 4m width, then the area of the plot is 16 sq.m. But if you are having a plot of 4m squared, then the area is 16 meters squared area.

What is Square Feet (Sqft)?

As India is having a vast population, so is the diversity of land measuring units. But under the Real Estate Regulatory Authority Act, all the lands including residential and commercial properties are to be measured in terms of Square Feet. It is a standard unit of measurement. Thus all the prices remain fixed and correct. No fraudulent activity can affect an individual or a broker.

A person who resides in the northern region of India can use Bigha, Biswa, Kanal and needs to convert Bigha to Square Feet, Biswa to Square Feet, and Kanal to Square Feet. 1 Bigha is equivalent to 14348.29 sq ft. 1 Biswa is equal to 1350 sq ft. And 1 Kanal is equal to 5445 sq ft. For the southern part of India, one needs to know about how to convert Guntha to Square Feet or Ground to Square Feet. 1 Guntha is equal to 1089 sq ft and 1 Ground is equivalent to 2400 sq ft. But values for local units keep changing from place to place and converting it into standard units will help to fix the price of a property

Current Use of Square Meter

Square meter helps in measuring the area of blocks or tracts of land, plots, homes, rooms and parking facilities among other uses.

Current Use of Square Feet

Square Feet are used for measuring land and available space for apartments, villas, independent houses, and residential plots along with commercial and office spaces.
