Relationship between MM and Inches


The relationship of millimeters to inches is quite simple indeed. One inch equates to 25.4 millimeters. A millimeter also works out to 0.0393701 inches. This is the standard relationship of mm to inches.

What is Millimeter?


A millimeter is a length measurement unit used within the SI (International System of Units). It is defined as 1/1000 of a meter or distance covered by light in 1/299 792 458 of a second. The meter has changed its definition over the years and the millimeter has also evolved likewise. Its relationship with the meter has remained the same and constant though. 

What is Inch?


An inch is a length measurement unit used within the U.S. and Imperial customary measurement systems. An inch works out to 25.4 millimeters as per the 1959 definition. A foot contains 12 inches while a yard contains 36 inches in all. Inch comes from uncia in Latin which works out to 1/12th of a single Roman foot. The inch has witnessed several definitions arising for it in the past years.
