Mahabhumi Abhilekh

The Maharashtra government has created this online land record portal for all the land records in the state of Maharashtra called Bhulekh Maha or Maha Bhumi Abhilekh. Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad, Nagpur, Konkan and Amravathi are the six major places. They divide the information on the portal according to the various needs of the citizens in relation to the land information.. People who are interested to know about the land in the state of Maharashtra can collect details with the help of this portal. This will save the time you spend outside the government offices and provides the information within a few minutes. 

What is this 7/12 extract information?

Every district in Maharashtra has its own land register. These registers contain details of all the lands in that particular district. The 7/12 is an extract from this type of register that holds the records and gives complete information and details required of a particular plot. This extract is issued by the revenue department of the state through the tahsildar. This register of records provides necessary information related to the ownership of the plot, occupancy, liabilities on the plot, survey number details, date of the ownership, etc. This also derives the details on the village form number. The number 7 in 7/12 indicates Form VII that has information of owners of the plot and their rights. The 12 in 7/12 stands for Form XII which provides information of all the agricultural features of the particular land. 

Form VII discloses extremely important data of record of rights, details of the occupants, details of the tenant, the land holders revenue obligations and other related details of the land. Form XII holds the information related to crops, the type of crops and the area cultivated by these crops. But one must note that they cannot transfer the property on the basis of 7/12 extract and cannot provide it as evidence for any legal purpose as it is not a conclusive document.

What is the satbara 7/12 extract?

The 7/12 gives detailed information on the history of ownership of a particular land. This will help people understand all the disputes that have come up in the past and will help to maintain clarity regarding the same and make sure to check if the disputes have been settled or not before any land transaction. This 7/12 extract also gives information about the crops grown on that land, types of crops and the usage of the land in detail. This helps in establishing information in a precise way to the people. 


How to receive the 7/12 extract online?

One way of getting this 7/12 extract information is by applying it to the tahsildar, mentioning the available description of the land, and clearly explaining the purpose of the need of that extract. 

The other and easier way is by applying through the official land record website of Maharashtra- Maha Online. One can get the information they are seeking, by entering the necessary details in the online portal as asked on the screen. Opting for a physical mode of application may be necessary in some cases when the details are not found online. 

The procedure to obtain 7/12 extract online on the official website is as follows:

Step 1: Visit the official Maha Bhulekh or Maharashtra Bhumi Abhilekh portal/ Maha Online Login.

https://bhulekh.mahabhumi.gov.in/

Step 2: Select the region of your choice from the drop-down menu.

Step 3: Once the page moves ahead, you will have to select the 7/12 option on the menu and then select the district from the drop-down list.

Step 4: Select the Taluka and the Village from the drop-down list.

Step 5: Now you can search the necessary information from your survey number, alphabetical survey number or group number, first name, name in, last name or full name.

Step 6: Once the detail submitted is appropriate, the requested information will pop up on screen and you will have to register yourself with the mobile number. Once this step gets approved, you will get the 7/12 extract information of your land.

How to apply for mutation (updation) in 7/12?

When the land owner finds any errors in the information that is depicted online or in the handwritten one of 7/12 extract, they must immediately apply for the correction of the same online. The errors may be related to: 

  • Total area of 7/12 extract
  • Unit of area
  • Name of the Account Holder
  • Area of the Account Holder

Once you identify the error, this is how you can apply for correction and updating of land records in Maharashtra. The steps are as follows:

Step 1: First you will have to visit the E-Rights portal and create an account.

https://pdeigr.maharashtra.gov.in/ 

Step 2: Once your account is created, you will have to login to the portal and select the ‘7/12 Mutations’ option to start the land record mutation entry.

Step 3: Then you will get a pop up to select the proper ‘ROLE’ of the user. The data entry can be done on three roles like:

  • Citizen
  • Bank/Society
  • Others 

The applicant has to be sure about their role and then only proceed through further steps as the mutation types are restricted for specific roles only. Once the role is selected by the person, they cannot go back and reselect other roles. So, the person must have proper knowledge about their role and then proceed with the filling of the details online.

Example:

The person with the CITIZEN role can do only the following tasks in that section:

  • Add Heirs
  • Remove the Name of the Guardian
  • Remove the HUF Name
  • Add/Remove a Will
  • Remove the Name of the Deceased Person
  • Change the Trustee Name 

Step 4: Here, you will have to submit all the details to register the changes that are made in the land records.

The present world functions around computers and smartphones. Accessing from phones is much easier than from computers. So a lot of apps take advantage of this easy accessibility. But people need to realise that not all the apps on Play Store are authentic. 

Land owners should be alert against the scam apps listed on Google Play Store as Maha Bhulekh. Land owners must note that there is NO OFFICIAL MOBILE APP of MahaBhulekh portal. All the information regarding 7/12 extract or 8A extract must be checked or verified only on the official website that is mentioned below:

https://bhulekh.mahabhumi.gov.in/

    The property cards provide information of the ownership of a property and history of holdings of land located in the urban areas in Maharashtra. But the 7/12 extract is the land record in the rural area that discloses various details of ownership of property, type and purpose of the land use and mainly depicts all the information outside the city limits.
