What is Centimeter?

A centimeter is a length measurement unit within the metric system used worldwide. It equals 1/100th of a meter. It also equates to 0.39370 inches in all. It is possibly one of the most widely used measurement units in several countries.

What is Foot?

The Foot is a widely used unit for length within the U.S. and Imperial systems of customary measurement. It indicates 1/3rd of a single yard while also being divided into 12 inches in total. 

Relation between Cm and Feet

The relationship between cm to feet is not hard to work out. 1 cm is equivalent to 0.0328084 feet. 1 feet is equivalent to  30.48 cm. 

How to Convert from Centimeter to Feet?

You can easily convert cm to ft. The modus operandi for converting cm to feet will be the following:

1 centimeter is 0.3937007874 feet. Suppose you have to convert 175 cm in feet, then you will have to divide 175 by 30.48, i.e. 5.741 feet. If it is a conversion for 185 cm in feet, then the answer will be 6.069 feet. 

Cm to Feet Formula & Examples

The formula for converting centimeters to feet is- ft = cm * 0.032808. Hence, if you are converting 168 cm in feet or 174 cm in feet, the answers will be 5.511 feet and 5.708 feet respectively. The answer for 157 cm in feet will be 5.150 feet.
